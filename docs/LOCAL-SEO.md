# Local SEO Checklist (GBP + Off-site)

Use this alongside on-site schema and content updates. **First-page rankings are not guaranteed** — especially for Ortaca, Göcek, and Fethiye queries where distance is a major Google factor.

## Google Business Profile (verified)

- [ ] Business name matches schema: **Diş Hekimi Tuğçe Aydın Çiğnaklı** (no keyword stuffing)
- [ ] Primary category: Dentist / Diş Hekimi (most specific available)
- [ ] Add secondary categories only if truly applicable (e.g. Cosmetic dentist, Dental implants)
- [ ] Address, phone (`+90 544 405 48 41`), hours match [src/lib/clinic.ts](../src/lib/clinic.ts) and site footer
- [ ] Website URL: `https://tugceaydincignakli.com`
- [ ] Appointment link: `https://tugceaydincignakli.com/tr/appointments?utm_source=gbp&utm_medium=referral&utm_campaign=appointment`
- [ ] Upload fresh interior, exterior, team, and treatment room photos monthly
- [ ] List all services offered (implant, whitening, checkup, etc.) in GBP services
- [ ] Respond to every review within 48 hours (professional, no medical details in replies)
- [ ] Post monthly updates (seasonal tips, new equipment, health tourism info)
- [ ] Cookiebot Distribution includes **Turkey** or **All visitors**

## NAP consistency (Name, Address, Phone)

Ensure identical values on:

- Website footer and contact page
- Google Business Profile
- Any professional directories or chamber listings you control

## Reviews (prominence signal)

- Ask satisfied patients organically after completed visits — never incentivize or fake reviews
- Aim for steady new reviews each month (recency matters)
- Do not copy-paste the same reply template for every review

## Citations & local links

- [ ] Turkish Dental Association / chamber listing if applicable
- [ ] Local business directories with correct NAP
- [ ] Health tourism pages only on reputable platforms (if used)

## What NOT to do

- Do not create separate city pages that only swap Ortaca/Göcek/Fethiye names (doorway risk)
- Do not buy backlinks or reviews
- Do not add fake `aggregateRating` schema on the website

## Monthly review (15 min)

1. Search Console → Performance → filter queries containing `dalaman`, `diş`, `implant`
2. GBP Insights → calls, direction requests, website clicks
3. GA4 → `phone_click`, `appointment_start`, `appointment_submit` conversions
4. Compare month-over-month; adjust content only when data supports it
